### Treton Puppet Board of Trustees
Early in the COVID-19 Pandemic, AADL needed to quickly move to online board meetings. I conceived of a Puppet Board Meeting as a way to practice. This event was super fun and quite successful, so in Winter 2021 we did 3 more Puppet Board Meetings, including live call-in segments from public puppet friends.

### Snub Cube
Originally a segment on The Saturday Show, Snub Cube was a puppet-hosted zoom game show with real kid contestants, pushing the limits of what zoom and OBS could do. It ran for 6 episodes. Hopefully we'll do Snub Cube Live some day!
